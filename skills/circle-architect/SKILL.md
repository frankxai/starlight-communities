# Circle Architect

Use this skill to propose Starlight creation cells.

## Inputs

- Member profiles.
- Week id.
- Theme.
- Consent flags.
- Blocked pairings, if any.

## Matching Rules

Hard constraints:

- 3-5 people per cell.
- Consent to cell matching.
- No blocked pairings.
- No private rationale surfaced to members.

Positive signals:

- Shared theme.
- Complementary skills.
- Compatible rhythm.
- Mixed but safe social energy.
- Clear artifact path for every member.

## Output

- Proposed cell map.
- Internal matching rationale.
- Member-safe explanation.
- Risks requiring steward review.
